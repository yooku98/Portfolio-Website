Inventory & Sales Analytics Dashboard

Problem

Limited visibility into sales and inventory performance.

Objective

Build a dashboard to support data-driven inventory and sales decisions.

Tools

Python, SQL, Tableau, Excel

Approach

  • Data cleaning and SQL querying
  • Exploratory data analysis
  • Dashboard design and KPI tracking

Key Insights

Identified sales trends, product performance, and inventory inefficiencies.

Impact

Improved decision-making and inventory planning.
